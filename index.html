SSDP GOTV 2018

This election season, Getting out the Youth Vote is more important than ever. We've set up phonebanks for two key states: Michigan and Utah. Our lists target young voters specifically. Clicking a state button will open a new tab in your browser to its respective statewide campaign. You'll have to register separately for each campaign for correct call tracking. This means if you register for Michigan's campaign, your login info will not work for Utah's, and you'll have to register again. You should only have to register once per campaign. Email tyler@ssdp.org if you have any problems with the phone bank.
